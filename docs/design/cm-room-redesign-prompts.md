# CM Room — Redesign Image Prompts

Five distinct visual directions for the CM Room redesign on `/brief`.
Render all at **21:9 ultra-wide** so the CM can compare like-for-like.

Maya's recommendation: **#2 (Palantir Gotham / Calm but Loaded).**
#1 is the original brief. #3–5 are deliberately different aesthetics
so the CM reacts to *range*, not variations of one idea.

The seven content slots referenced across all variants:
NEWS ON CM, OPPOSITION SIGNAL, RECOMMENDED ACTIONS, MONITOR LIST,
THREAT MATRIX, FUTURE OUTLOOK, plus a live Telangana map.

---

## 1. Live Monitor / SOC (original brief)

Ultra-wide 21:9 dashboard UI mockup. Left half: Telangana state map on
dark slate background (#0a0e1a), districts outlined in cyan with
glowing red/amber/green event pins, animated radar-sweep overlay,
district-level heat layer. Right half: dense 6-card grid with sharp
borders, neon accent tickers, sparklines, sentiment gauges, mini-bar
charts — cards labeled NEWS ON CM, OPPOSITION SIGNAL, RECOMMENDED
ACTIONS, MONITOR LIST, THREAT MATRIX, FUTURE OUTLOOK. Scrolling live
ticker bottom rail. Thin top nav with "CM ROOM" wordmark, alert pills,
system clock. IBM Plex Mono data type. Inspiration: Bloomberg Terminal
× NORAD ops × Live Monitor World Scope. Maximum information density,
every pixel meaningful.

---

## 2. Palantir Gotham / "Calm but Loaded" *(Maya's pick)*

Ultra-wide 21:9 dashboard UI mockup. Telangana map dominates left 60%,
dark navy background (#0d1117), districts as soft muted-teal polygons,
event pins as small refined dots with hairline connector lines to side
annotations — no radar gimmicks. Right 40%: vertical priority stack —
one large hero card on top labeled "WHAT CHANGED" with a single sharp
insight, three collapsed cards below (OPPOSITION, THREATS, OUTLOOK)
showing only headline plus delta. Generous whitespace, hairline 1px
dividers, Inter for UI, IBM Plex Mono for data. Thin live-event rail at
bottom. 90% greys and navy, accents only on signal (one amber, one
red). Reference: Palantir Gotham × Linear × Stripe dashboard. Calm,
loaded, executive.

---

## 3. Editorial / Premium Newsroom

Ultra-wide 21:9 dashboard UI mockup, light theme. Cream background
(#faf8f3), subtle paper texture. Left 50%: Telangana map as vintage
topographic engraving — cross-hatched, sepia, hand-drawn cartography
style — with serif annotations and small sepia event markers. Right
50%: editorial-style cards with large serif display headlines (Tiempos
or Canela), pull-quote callouts, generous line-height, desk-style
metadata ("OPPOSITION DESK," "THREAT DESK"). Top masthead reads "THE
CHIEF MINISTER'S BRIEF" in large serif with dateline beneath.
Reference: The Economist Espresso × NYT The Morning × FT Weekend
digital. Sophisticated, intellectual, executive briefing.

---

## 4. Glassmorphic / Cinematic Premium

Ultra-wide 21:9 cinematic dashboard mockup. Hero background: 3D
topographic Telangana map with soft elevation shading, gradient sky
from deep indigo to warm magenta at horizon. Foreground: floating
frosted-glass cards with soft drop shadows, depth-of-field blur on
distant ones, asymmetric layered composition — large hero card
center-left with live sentiment gauge, smaller satellite cards (NEWS,
OPPOSITION, THREATS, MONITOR, OUTLOOK) orbiting at varied depths. SF
Pro Display type, refined tracking, subtle particle motion implied.
Reference: Apple Vision Pro UI × Arc Browser × Tesla cockpit. Premium,
futuristic, luxe.

---

## 5. War Room Tactical / Amber CRT

Ultra-wide 21:9 dashboard mockup. Aesthetic: tactical operations room,
aged paper map meets amber CRT terminal. Telangana rendered as
hand-drawn tactical chart on khaki paper, stenciled district names, red
wax-pencil annotations, small flag pins. Right side: amber-on-black
terminal cards with monospaced VT323 type, scanline overlay, blinking
cursor accents, classified banding ("BRIEF // CM-EYES-ONLY"). Cards
labeled INTEL, OPPOSITION MOVEMENT, ACTION ORDERS, WATCHLIST, THREATS,
FORECAST. Typewriter-style ticker bottom. Subtle CRT curvature at
edges. Reference: Mossad ops room × Fallout terminal × Severance ×
Apollo mission control. Commanding, serious, cinematic.
